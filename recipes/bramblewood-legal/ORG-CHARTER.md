Bramblewood Legal is a five-person real-estate practice: a Managing
Partner who signs the engagement letters, two attorneys and a paralegal
who run the matters, and a Records Clerk who keeps the file room and the
books. Matter confidentiality is the house religion; the client folder is
readable by the matter team and the Managing Partner, nobody else, and the
firm's PERMISSIONS.md is treated as seriously as any pleading. Documents
read like a careful small firm wrote them: engagement letters with defined
scopes and signature blocks, kickoff memos that list the parcels and
filings in play, working-session minutes naming every attendee, and status
reports a client's general counsel can forward upward without a call.
Tone is precise and unhurried; dates and dollar figures appear exactly
where the record requires them and nowhere else.

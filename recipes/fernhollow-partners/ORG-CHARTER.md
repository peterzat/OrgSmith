Fernhollow Partners is a five-person financial advisory boutique serving
founder-owned and sponsor-backed operating companies through valuations,
transaction readiness, and post-close work. The relationship runs long:
client finance leads move between employers mid-history and bring
Fernhollow with them, so the same outside contact may sign an engagement
letter under one firm and appear in minutes under another years later.
Engagement letters go out as signed PDFs over the Managing Partner's
name, kickoff memos and status reports live in Word, board-ready
readouts are short PowerPoint decks, and scheduling happens in concise
email threads. Tone is measured, numerate business English: figures
carry the argument, adjectives do not; first names inside the firm,
full names and titles when a client is on the page. Spreadsheets are
annual, tidy, and tie out.

Gladepoint Strategies is a five-person strategy consultancy founded in
2020, working with mid-market software and industrial clients. The firm
runs lean, distributed, and calendar-driven: engagement letters go out as
signed PDFs, kickoff memos and status reports live in Word, steering
updates are short PowerPoint decks presented over video calls, and a lot
of the actual coordination happens in brisk email threads between the
engagement lead, the client sponsor, and the team. The Managing Director
signs the letters; principals run the work. Tone is contemporary,
plainspoken business English: short paragraphs, concrete verbs, first
names internally, full names and titles when a client is on the page.
Spreadsheets are tidy and annual.
